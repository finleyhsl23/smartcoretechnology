The Sales Script

What to say, and when to say it

Use this as a conversation guide, not a script to read verbatim. The goal is to get them talking about their current pain, then show them how SmartCore solves it specifically.

Opening line — first 60 seconds

"Most businesses we work with are running on a combination of spreadsheets, email, and maybe a basic CRM that doesn't really talk to anything else. The problem isn't just the chaos — it's that nothing is visible. Your leads are in one place, your quotes are in your inbox, your support tickets are in your head. SmartCore brings all of that under one roof, and because it's built by us specifically, we can adapt it to the way you work — not the other way around."

Discovery questions to ask first

Find their pain before pitching

  • How are you currently tracking your leads and follow-ups?
  • When a new enquiry comes in, what happens to it?
  • Do you have visibility of what's in your pipeline right now?
  • How do you send quotes — are they consistent, professional-looking?
  • How long does it take from quote to signed agreement?
  • When a customer has an issue, how do they reach you?
  • Do you have any idea which of your team is performing best?
Common pain points we solve

Listen for these triggers

  • "We lose track of enquiries" → Lead pipeline & reminders
  • "Quotes take too long to get out" → Quote builder + e-signature
  • "Customers chase us for updates" → Customer portal
  • "I don't know what my team is doing" → Tasks, calendar, leaderboard
  • "We're spending a fortune on Salesforce" → Price comparison
  • "Support requests come in from everywhere" → Tickets + messaging
  • "We want to send newsletters / offers" → Newsletter tool
Handling Objections
🤔

"We already have a CRM — HubSpot / Salesforce / Monday."

💬

"That's great — those are well-known tools. The question is whether you're actually using all of it, and whether you're paying for features you don't need. HubSpot's CRM starts free but the moment you want automation, quotes, or a proper portal, you're looking at hundreds a month — sometimes over a thousand. SmartCore gives you a complete system from £19.99, with e-signatures, a customer portal, and AI baked in. And if you want anything custom, you deal with a real UK team, not a support ticket that takes 3 days."

🤔

"It seems quite small — is it enterprise-grade?"

💬

"Enterprise-grade doesn't mean complicated — it means reliable, secure, and scalable. SmartCore runs on Cloudflare's global edge network and Supabase (PostgreSQL) — the same infrastructure used by companies processing millions of requests a day. Being a smaller provider works in your favour: you're not a ticket number, and changes that would take Salesforce 6 months to configure take us days."

🤔

"What if we need something specific that you don't have?"

💬

"That's exactly the conversation we want to have. Unlike off-the-shelf CRMs, SmartCore is built to be extended. Tell us what you need — a specific workflow, a custom report, an integration — and we can discuss adding it. We built an AI chat assistant, e-signature flow, and leaderboard for our existing customers because they asked for it. Your feedback shapes the product."

🤔

"We're worried about data security / GDPR."

💬

"Absolutely fair concern. Your data is stored in isolated database schemas — your records are never mixed with another company's. All traffic runs over HTTPS with enterprise-grade encryption at rest. We're a UK company operating under UK/EU data protection law, and we can provide a data processing agreement on request. We don't sell data, and we never have."

🤔

"Can we try it before we commit?"

💬

"Yes — we can get a demo environment set up with your branding and some dummy data so you can actually click around in something that feels like yours. The best way to sell it is to show it, not describe it. Can we book 30 minutes this week?"

How to get them over the line

The Close Sequence

1

Demo first, always

Don't try to close without a demo. The product sells itself once they see it live. Set up a screen share, log in as an admin, show the pipeline, create a quote in front of them, send it to their email, and have them open it on their phone. That moment — when they sign it on their own screen — closes more deals than any feature list.

2

Confirm the right tier together

"Based on what you've told me, I'd recommend Professional at £39.99 — you get quotes, e-signature, reports, and the leaderboard. That's probably going to replace at least one or two tools you're currently paying for separately. Does that feel about right?"

3

Do the maths out loud

If they're on HubSpot Sales Hub Professional, they're paying £441/month. SmartCore Professional is £39.99. That's a saving of over £400/month — nearly £5,000 a year — for a system that does more. Say that number out loud. "You'd save around £400 a month switching to us. That pays for a lot."

4

Remove the risk

"We can get you set up with your branding and a trial period so you're not committing blind. If after 30 days it's not working for your team, we'll talk about it. But in our experience, once the team is using it, they don't want to go back."

5

The specific ask

Don't leave without a next step. "Can I put you down for an onboarding call this week? I'll have your branding set up before we get on the call so you can log in and it already looks like yours." Specific beats vague — "let me know" closes nothing.
